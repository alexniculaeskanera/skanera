Solutions
Built for two high-value verticals

Skanera serves insurance companies and storage facility operators — the two industries that benefit most from documented, photo-verified home inventories.

🛡️

Insurance Companies

Offer Skanera to policyholders as a value-added service. Photo-documented inventories mean faster, fairer claims — reducing fraud and dispute costs while improving customer satisfaction.

Ideal for: home insurance, renters insurance, contents coverage, storage unit riders.

Per-policy licensing · Volume discounts available
🏢

Storage Facilities

White-label Skanera under your brand and offer it to customers storing belongings in your facility. Increase unit utilisation, reduce disputes, and differentiate your service.

Ideal for: self-storage, climate-controlled storage, moving & storage companies.

Per-unit pricing · Full white-label included
🏘️

Property Management

Equip tenants with Skanera as part of their move-in package. Documented inventories protect both landlords and renters, reduce move-out disputes, and add a tangible perk.

Ideal for: residential property managers, condo corporations, student housing.

Per-tenant pricing · API integration available
