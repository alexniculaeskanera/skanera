How it works
From cluttered bin to smart inventory in minutes

No typing, no spreadsheets. Just photos — and AI does the rest.

Step 01
📷

Photograph your items

Snap photos of everything inside your box. No setup needed — just point and shoot.

Step 02
🤖

AI labels everything

Skanera's AI instantly identifies each item and names it. Edit any label with a tap.

Step 03
🔲

Print a QR sticker

Generate a custom QR label. Print on sticker paper, stick it on the box — done.

Step 04
🔍

Find anything, fast

Search "plastic forks" and Skanera shows the exact box and room. Or scan the QR to browse everything inside.
